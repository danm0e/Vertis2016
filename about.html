About

Providing sophisticated solutions in private aviation

Vertis Aviation is a unique boutique practice with unparalleled experience and commercial creativity. We deliver through exclusive direct charter of our own luxury fleet or through our ability to negotiate innovative charter brokerage contracts.

The Vertis fleet is an optimised portfolio of premium private aviation heavy jet aircraft types and includes an outstanding example of the Airbus Corporate Jet. Vertis provides a bespoke approach for select clients whose needs match the design of our aircraft fleet in terms of profile, positioning and availability. We also offer independent private aviation consultancy and intelligent aircraft management and marketing.

Vertis Aviation is a subsidiary of 28 East Group. For more information, visit the 28 East website.
